Black Heritage Future Leaders 2024

Have you heard of Black Heritage Future Leaders (BHFL) by Bright Network? Hosted yearly, BHFL is an event where students of black heritage can attend and hear from senior leaders who can help navigate having a career as a black professional. This is especially important as it is good to hear form those who are in their respective fields, and hear any advise they have. One reason why I applied was due to the opportunity to network with fellow black students / graduates on the Guild platform and LinkedIn. Furthermore, It is great to hear about diversity and inclusion programmes and hear what companies are doing to be inclusive from the perspective of employers.

Also, after the event, you earn a certificate which looks great on your CV! Interested? Click on the link below!

The 2% project

Founded in and hosted in 2022 by Badu Mentoring Group and the Black Excellence Network, Project 2% gives black undergraduates the opportunity to learn from black leaders who are thriving in their respective industries. The primary goal of project 2% is to provide a dynamic platform for black young individuals where they can aquire invaluable insights that will empower them to thrive in their chosen field.

The name came from a discovery made in 2020 that black people held less than 2% of leadership roles across a range of industries.

The conference includes: Masterclasses - These masterclasses are hosted by black leaders and attendees can choose which masterclass to attend based on the industry represented. this enables attendees to gain enlightenment on the black experience in various professional positions. Furthermore, there is the opportunity for attendees to ask questions. Keynote speakers - They provide exclusive wisdom on to excel in their chosen fields and advise on how to step into underrepresented fields. Opportunities - This includes the opportunity to connect with sponsor firms and market stall companies. these companies can offer bespoke opportunities to attendees.

Couch to Coder

Join Bright Network's Couch 2 Coder course. This 5 week course enables you to learn how to code your very own website using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Got no time to finish to the course on the day of release? No problem! The lessons are pre recorded meaning that you have the whole week to ensure that you are up to date with the lesson and tasks before the next week. This course also touches on the use of AI when coding and even touches on the history of AI.

Never coded before? No problem, join today and add another skill set to your resume!
